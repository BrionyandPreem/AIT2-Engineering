History & Milestones

2002

The Foundation

AIT Engineering and Service Co., Ltd. was established in September 2002. Our core mission from day one has been to become Thailand's leading instrument and service provider for Field Instruments, Analyzers, Transmitters, and Actuation Technologies across diverse industrial sectors. We committed ourselves to providing high-quality engineering and services to meet the rapidly growing demands of our customers.

2006

Strategic Expansion

2005: Opened a new office in the Banpong area, strategically located near SCG Packaging, to enhance our service response time and support local industrial operations.

2006: Achieved a significant milestone by being appointed by Emerson Process Management as the official local business partner specializing in the Pulp and Paper industry.

2012

Regional Growth

2010: Expanded our footprint to Northeast Thailand by establishing a dedicated office in Khon Kaen, ensuring comprehensive support for customers in the region.

2012: Entered the Eastern Economic Corridor by renting an office in Rayong, focusing on the sales and service of high-performance valves and actuators.

2015

Standard & Excellence

In 2013, we began implementing ISO Standards across all company operations to ensure global-level service quality. This was followed by applying for the Bureau Veritas Seal in 2014.

By 2015, we successfully passed the Bureau Veritas Certification for Sales, Installation, and After-sales services. In the same year, we relocated our Rayong office to its current location, featuring a brand new 500 sq.m professional workshop.

2020

Global Reach

AIT continues to build international trust. We signed official partnership contracts with PMT S.r.l in 2018 and Airaghi S.r.l. in 2020, further strengthening our portfolio with world-class industrial solutions.
